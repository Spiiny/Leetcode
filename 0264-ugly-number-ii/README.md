<h2><a href="https://leetcode.com/problems/ugly-number-ii">264. Ugly Number II</a></h2>
<h3>Medium</h3>
<hr>

<p>An <strong>ugly number</strong> is a positive integer whose prime factors are limited to 2, 3, and 5.</p>

<p>Given an integer <code>n</code>, return the <code>n<sup>th</sup></code> ugly number.</p>

<hr>

<p><strong>Example 1:</strong></p>

<pre>
Input: n = 10
Output: 12

Explanation:
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12] is the sequence of the first 10 ugly numbers.
</pre>

<p><strong>Example 2:</strong></p>

<pre>
Input: n = 1
Output: 1

Explanation:
1 has no prime factors, therefore all of its prime factors are limited to 2, 3, and 5.
</pre>

<hr>

<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><code>1 &lt;= n &lt;= 1690</code></li>
</ul>

<hr>

<p><strong>Function Signature:</strong></p>

<pre>
int nthUglyNumber(int n);
</pre>
